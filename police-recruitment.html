About Police Recruitment Training

The Police Recruitment program at The Champions Academy prepares candidates for Maharashtra Police Constable, Armed Police Constable, and State Reserve Police Force (SRPF) examinations. Our structured training covers both the written examination and physical fitness requirements with equal rigor.

🏃

Physical Training

Daily running, long jump, high jump, and endurance drills by ex-defense trainers.

📝

Written Exam Prep

Comprehensive coverage of Marathi, English, Maths, and General Knowledge.

Syllabus & Core Modules

Our curriculum is meticulously designed to cover every topic from the official Police Recruitment syllabus, ensuring thorough preparation.

  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
  • Quantitative Aptitude & Reasoning
  • Marathi & English Language Proficiency
  • Physical Efficiency Tests (Running, Jumping)
  • Previous year paper analysis & mock tests
