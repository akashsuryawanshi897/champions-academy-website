About Forest Service Training

The Forest Service Recruitment Training Program at The Champions Academy is tailored for candidates seeking positions as Vanrakshak (Forest Guard) and Vansevak (Forest Worker) in the Maharashtra Forest Department. Our holistic approach combines rigorous physical conditioning with focused academic preparation.

🌲

Field Training

Outdoor terrain training, forest navigation, and wildlife awareness modules.

🏃

Physical Endurance

1600m/800m running, long jump, and high jump — exceeding cutoff standards.

Syllabus & Core Modules

Our curriculum covers the full Forest Department recruitment syllabus including environmental science, general knowledge, and physical tests.

  • General Knowledge & Maharashtra Geography
  • Forest Laws & Environmental Science
  • Marathi Language & Comprehension
  • Physical Fitness Training (Running, Jumping)
  • Wildlife Awareness & Forest Conservation
